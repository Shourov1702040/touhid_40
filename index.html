Overview

I am currently a Lecturer (Assistant Professor) at Deakin University, School of Information Technologies, Burwood, Melbourne. Before that, I was postdoctoral research fellow at RMIT University, Melbourne. I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science at RMIT University under the supervision of Professor Zahir Tari. Prior to that, I earned a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering (Software) and an Honours degree in Computer Science.
